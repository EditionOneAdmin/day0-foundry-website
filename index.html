FAQ

Common questions answered.

How much equity do I give up?
Equity split depends on the venture and your contribution. Typically, solopreneurs retain significant ownership (30-50%) while Day-0 Foundry holds the majority. As you hit milestones, you can earn additional equity.
Do I need a specific idea to apply?
No. We often match talented operators with ideas from our pipeline. If you have an idea, great. If not, your execution skills matter more than your ideas.
What happens after 90 days?
If the venture shows traction (customers, revenue, growth), we scale it with additional funding and team members. If not, we kill it quickly and you can either take on a new venture or exit the program.
Can I work on this part-time?
No. Day-0 requires full commitment. 90 days is not a lot of time to build a company. We need your full focus and energy.
Where are you based?
We're remote-first with a hub in Berlin. Our solopreneurs work from anywhere, but we gather regularly for sprints and strategy sessions.
